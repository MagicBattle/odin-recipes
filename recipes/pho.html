Pho

Image of Pho

Pho is a process that can take a long time due to the setup process for the broth.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg beef bones
  • 1 large onion
  • 4-inch ginger
  • 5 star anise pods
  • 4 cloves
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 1 tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tbsp fennel seeds
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 500g beef brisket, thinly sliced
  • 400g rice noodles
  • Fresh herbs (cilantro, basil, mint)
  • Lime wedges, bean sprouts, chili

Steps

  1. Boil bones for 5 mins; rinse.
  2. Char onion and ginger; toast spices.
  3. Simmer bones, onion, ginger, spices, fish sauce, and sugar for 3-4 hours.
  4. Strain the broth.
  5. Cook noodles; rinse.
  6. Assemble with noodles, beef, and broth.
  7. Garnish with herbs, lime, and sprouts.
